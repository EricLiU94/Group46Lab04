Two or More Dependent Variables

In the second example, the object of scrutiny, Iris dataset, contains a number of columns and 150 observation points. When attempting to calculate the regressions between two or more independent variables and a dependent variable, the formula should be declared in a slightly different fashion.

formula <- Petal.Length~Sepal.Width+Sepal.Length

The computation could be initialised by typing:

x<-linreg(formula= Petal.Length~Sepal.Width+Sepal.Length, data=iris)
